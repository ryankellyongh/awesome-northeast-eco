## Energy

- [Mass Save](https://www.masssave.com/) - Massachusetts energy efficiency program offering rebates and incentives for insulation, heat pumps, and appliances. One of the most generous state programs in the country.
- [NY Green Bank](https://greenbank.ny.gov/) - New York state-sponsored investment fund that accelerates clean energy deployment by financing projects that traditional lenders won't.
- [Eversource Green Options](https://www.eversource.com/) - Renewable energy options for Connecticut, Massachusetts, and New Hampshire customers.
- [Green Mountain Power](https://greenmountainpower.com/) - Vermont's largest electric utility, with a strong focus on renewable energy and innovative programs like battery storage for customers.
- [ReVision Energy](https://revisionenergy.com/) - Employee-owned solar and heat pump installer operating across Maine, New Hampshire, and Massachusetts.
- [Community Choice Aggregation (CCA)](https://www.sierraclub.org/massachusetts/community-choice-aggregation) - Many Northeast municipalities offer CCA programs letting residents collectively purchase greener electricity. Search for your town's program.
- [UK Carbon Intensity API equivalent — ISO-NE Grid](https://www.iso-ne.com/isoexpress/) - Monitor real-time generation mix for the New England grid to see when electricity is cleanest.

## Food

- [Boston Area Gleaners](https://www.bostonareagleaners.org/) - Rescues surplus crops from farms across eastern New England and distributes them to food banks.
- [City Growers (NYC)](https://citygrowers.org/) - Urban farm education and food access programs across New York City.
- [Farm Fresh Rhode Island](https://www.farmfreshri.org/) - Connects Rhode Island residents to local farms through markets, CSAs, and gleaning programs.
- [GrowNYC](https://www.grownyc.org/) - Operates over 50 greenmarkets and food scrap drop-off sites across New York City.
- [Imperfect Foods](https://www.imperfectfoods.com/) - Delivers "cosmetically imperfect" produce that would otherwise go to waste. Available across the Northeast.
- [Provisions](https://www.provisionsnaturalfoods.com/) - Zero-waste grocery delivery in select New England markets.
- [Too Good To Go](https://toogoodtogo.com/en-us) - App for rescuing surplus food from restaurants and cafes at a discount. Active in Boston, NYC, and other Northeast cities.
- [Willing Hands](https://www.willinghands.org/) - Vermont food rescue organization collecting and distributing surplus food from farms, restaurants, and retailers.

## Finance

- [Amalgamated Bank](https://www.amalgamatedbank.com/) - NYC-based certified B Corp with no fossil fuel lending, powered by 100% renewable energy.
- [Beneficial State Bank](https://beneficialstatebank.com/) - Mission-driven bank that does not fund fossil fuel extraction. B Corp and FDIC member.
- [Coastal Heritage Bank](https://www.coastalheritagebank.com/) - Massachusetts community bank with a sustainability focus.
- [Eastern Bank](https://www.easternbank.com/) - Boston-based mutual bank with strong community and environmental commitments.
- [NH Community Loan Fund](https://www.nhclf.org/) - Community development financial institution offering green loans for energy efficiency upgrades in New Hampshire.
- [Clean Energy Capital](https://www.cleanenergycapital.com/) - NYC-based firm financing commercial clean energy projects across the region.
- [Connecticut Green Bank](https://www.ctgreenbank.com/) - State-level green bank offering financing for solar, efficiency, and clean energy for CT homeowners and businesses.
- [NY Green Bank](https://greenbank.ny.gov/) - See [Energy](#energy) section above.

## Transportation

- [Bluebikes](https://www.bluebikes.com/) - Boston-area bike share covering Boston, Cambridge, Somerville, and surrounding towns.
- [Citi Bike](https://citibikenyc.com/) - NYC's large bike share network, one of the biggest in the US.
- [Green Commuter](https://www.greencommuter.com/) - Electric van pool service operating in the Northeast, replacing single-occupancy commutes.
- [MassDOT EV Charging](https://www.mass.gov/electric-vehicles) - State resources for EV charging infrastructure, rebates, and incentives in Massachusetts.
- [NY Charge](https://nycharge.ny.gov/) - New York State EV charging network and incentive programs.
- [MBTA](https://www.mbta.com/) - Boston's public transit system. Taking the T instead of driving is one of the highest-impact switches you can make.
- [Ride Amtrak's Northeast Corridor](https://www.amtrak.com/northeast-regional-train) - Train travel between DC, Philadelphia, NYC, Providence, and Boston generates a fraction of the emissions of flying or driving alone.
- [Zagster / Gotcha](https://gotcha.bike/) - E-bike and bike share programs operating in smaller Northeast cities and college towns.

## Home Improvements

- [CT Energize](https://www.ctenergize.com/) - Connecticut's statewide energy efficiency program with rebates for insulation, heat pumps, and more.
- [EmPower New York](https://www.nyserda.ny.gov/All-Programs/EmPower-New-York-Program) - Free energy efficiency upgrades for low-income New York households via NYSERDA.
- [Efficiency Maine](https://www.efficiencymaine.com/) - Rebates and financing for heat pumps, EVs, weatherization, and more for Maine residents.
- [Efficiency Vermont](https://www.efficiencyvermont.com/) - The nation's first statewide energy efficiency utility, offering rebates and technical assistance.
- [Mass Save Heat Pump Rebates](https://www.masssave.com/residential/rebates-and-incentives/heating-and-cooling/) - Up to $10,000 in rebates for cold-climate heat pumps in Massachusetts.
- [NJ Clean Energy Program](https://www.njcleanenergy.com/) - New Jersey's statewide program for renewable energy and efficiency upgrades with cash rebates.
- [NY State Home Energy Assessment](https://www.nyserda.ny.gov/All-Programs/Home-Energy-Audit) - Free or subsidized home energy audits to identify the best improvements for your home.
- [Rhode Island Energy Efficiency](https://rienergy.com/save-money-energy/rebates-incentives/) - Rebates and programs for Rhode Island residents from the state utility.

## Waste & Recycling

- [Big Reuse (NYC)](https://bigreuse.org/) - Deconstructs buildings and resells salvaged materials. Also accepts donations of building materials and household goods.
- [Boston Building Resources](https://bostonbuildingresources.com/) - Sells salvaged and surplus building materials at low cost in the Boston area.
- [GrowNYC Food Scrap Drop-Off](https://www.grownyc.org/compost) - Interactive map of 150+ food scrap drop-off locations across NYC.
- [Litterati](https://www.litterati.org/) - App to photograph, tag, and track litter pickup. Active community of cleaners in Northeast cities.
- [Repair Cafés Northeast](https://www.repaircafe.org/en/visit/) - Find a local Repair Café where volunteers help fix clothes, electronics, bikes, and more for free.
- [ReSET (Hartford)](https://resetct.org/) - Connecticut social enterprise hub focused on building a more circular, equitable local economy.
- [TerraCycle](https://www.terracycle.com/) - Mail-in recycling programs for hard-to-recycle items like chip bags, pens, and cosmetics. Drop-off locations across the Northeast.
- [Vermont Solid Waste Districts](https://dec.vermont.gov/waste-management/solid-waste/districts) - Vermont's network of solid waste districts, many offering composting, e-waste, and hazardous waste collection.

## Carbon Reduction

- [Acadia Center](https://acadiacenter.org/) - Northeast-focused nonprofit pushing for clean energy policies across New England and the Mid-Atlantic.
- [Climate XChange](https://climate-xchange.org/) - Boston-based org advancing carbon pricing and climate policy in the Northeast.
- [Ecologi](https://ecologi.com/) - Monthly subscription to offset your footprint and fund reforestation. Available globally, widely used in the US.
- [Project Wren](https://www.wren.co/) - US-based carbon offset subscription with transparent project tracking.
- [Tradewater](https://tradewater.us/) - Chicago-originated but nationally active: finds and destroys potent refrigerant gases before they escape into the atmosphere. Verified offsets available.

- [Acadia Center Jobs](https://acadiacenter.org/about/employment/) - Policy, research, and outreach roles at a leading Northeast clean energy nonprofit.
- [ClimateCareer.us](https://climatecareer.us/) - Job board focused on climate and clean energy roles, many in the Northeast.
- [Green Jobs Network](https://www.greenjobs.net/) - Broad green job board covering sustainability roles across all sectors in the US.
- [MassCEC Jobs](https://www.masscec.com/jobs) - Job listings at the Massachusetts Clean Energy Center and its partner companies.
- [NYSERDA Careers](https://www.nyserda.ny.gov/About/Careers) - Roles at New York's energy research and development authority.
- [ReVision Energy Careers](https://revisionenergy.com/careers/) - Jobs at a leading employee-owned solar and heat pump installer in the Northeast.

## Volunteering

- [350 Mass](https://350mass.org/) - Grassroots climate action group with chapters across Massachusetts.
- [Appalachian Mountain Club](https://www.outdoors.org/get-involved/volunteer/) - Trail maintenance and conservation volunteering across the Northeast.
- [Citizens Climate Lobby – Northeast Chapters](https://citizensclimatelobby.org/chapters/) - Find your local chapter for policy-focused climate advocacy.
- [Clean Air Task Force](https://www.catf.us/) - Boston-based org working on clean air and climate policy. Volunteer and action opportunities.
- [Clean Ocean Access (RI)](https://www.cleanoceanaccess.org/) - Rhode Island organization running beach cleanups and ocean conservation programs.
- [Extinction Rebellion – Northeast](https://xrebellion.org/) - Find your local XR chapter for direct action and community organizing.
- [Save the Sound](https://www.savethesound.org/) - Protects Long Island Sound and its watersheds through advocacy, restoration, and citizen science.
- [Sunrise Movement – Northeast Hubs](https://www.sunrisemovement.org/hubs/) - Youth-led climate movement with active hubs across the Northeast.

## News & Media

- [Canary Media](https://www.canarymedia.com/) - Independent journalism covering the clean energy transition in the US.
- [Commonwealth Beacon](https://commonwealthbeacon.org/) - Massachusetts-focused public interest journalism including energy and environment coverage.
- [Energy News Network – Northeast](https://energynews.us/northeast/) - Regional clean energy news aggregator covering New England and the Mid-Atlantic.
- [Inside Climate News](https://insideclimatenews.org/) - Pulitzer Prize-winning outlet covering climate science and policy across the US and globally.
- [The Narwhal](https://thenarwhal.ca/) - Independent investigative journalism on environmental issues in North America.

## Politics & Advocacy

- [Acadia Center](https://acadiacenter.org/) - See [Carbon Reduction](#carbon-reduction). Also does significant policy advocacy work across the region.
- [Environment America – Northeast](https://environmentamerica.org/) - State-level chapters across the Northeast pushing for clean energy, clean water, and open spaces.
- [MASSPIRG](https://masspirg.org/) - Massachusetts consumer and environmental advocacy organization.
- [NYPIRG](https://www.nypirg.org/) - New York's equivalent, with strong environmental programs.
- [Northeast Clean Energy Council](https://www.necec.org/) - Industry and advocacy group advancing clean energy policy across New England.
- [Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative (RGGI)](https://www.rggi.org/) - Multi-state carbon cap-and-trade program covering Connecticut, Delaware, Maine, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, New York, Rhode Island, Vermont, and Virginia. A major policy lever for the region.
