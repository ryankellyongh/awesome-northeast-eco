# Contributing to Awesome Northeast Eco

Thanks for helping make this list better! Here's how to contribute.

## What belongs here

- Organizations, tools, services, or programs that are:
  - Based in or specifically serving New England / the Northeast US (CT, MA, ME, NH, NJ, NY, RI, VT)
  - Actively operating (check that the link works and the org is current)
  - Genuinely focused on sustainability, clean energy, food systems, waste reduction, or related topics

## What doesn't belong here

- General national resources with no specific Northeast relevance (those are better suited for lists like [awesome-earth](https://github.com/philsturgeon/awesome-earth))
- Commercial products without a clear sustainability angle
- Anything you haven't verified is still active

## How to submit a PR

1. Fork this repository
2. Add your entry to the relevant section in `README.md`, in alphabetical order
3. Use this format:
   ```
   - [Name](https://url.com) - One or two sentence description you wrote yourself. Don't copy from the org's website.
   ```
4. Open a pull request with a short explanation of what you're adding and why it belongs

## Style guide

- Write descriptions in your own words
- Keep descriptions to 1–2 sentences
- Use sentence case for descriptions (not Title Case)
- Confirm the URL loads correctly before submitting
